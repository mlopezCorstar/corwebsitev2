About Corstar Communications

Serving Westchester County businesses since 1970. More than five decades of technology partnerships, built on reliability and honest work.

Our Story

55 Years and Still Answering the Phone

Corstar Communications was founded in 1970 in Westchester County, NY. We started by helping local businesses manage their communications infrastructure — back when that meant telephone systems and copper wiring. Over the decades, we evolved alongside our clients, growing from a cabling and communications company into a full-service IT Managed Service Provider.

What hasn't changed is our belief that technology should work for your business — not the other way around. We don't oversell complexity. We don't disappear after the sale. We are your local IT department, and we take that seriously.

Today, Corstar manages the technology for over 200 businesses across Westchester County, from solo medical practices to multi-location manufacturing firms. We've outlasted dozens of competitors because we do the work right and we're honest with our clients.
