IT for Legal Professionals

Your Clients' Confidentiality Depends on Your IT

Law firms hold some of the most sensitive data that exists — privileged communications, financial records, personal information, and strategic business intelligence. A breach doesn't just create a liability — it can end client relationships and trigger bar complaints.

Westchester law firms are a high-value target. Business email compromise (BEC) attacks that redirect wire transfers have cost law firms millions. Ransomware attacks can lock you out of case files during a trial. These are not hypothetical scenarios — they happen regularly to firms that thought they were too small to be targeted.

Corstar understands the legal environment. We've supported Westchester law firms for decades, from solo practitioners to multi-attorney practices.
