Manufacturing IT

IT for the Plant Floor and the Front Office

Manufacturing businesses operate two distinct technology environments: the IT side (office systems, email, ERP, accounting) and the OT (operational technology) side (PLCs, SCADA, HMIs, production equipment). These environments used to be completely separate — but today they are increasingly connected, and that creates significant security and uptime risks.

Corstar serves Westchester manufacturing businesses that need an IT partner who understands both sides. We don't treat your plant floor like an office building — we know the difference between a Windows server running your ERP and a Windows XP machine running a CNC controller that can't be patched.

Our priority for manufacturing clients is maximum uptime. Every decision we make — from network design to backup strategy — is made through the lens of keeping your production running.

The OT Security Problem
