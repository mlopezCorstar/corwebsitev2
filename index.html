Answers to Your IT Questions

A Managed Service Provider (MSP) is a company that remotely manages your IT systems and infrastructure. Instead of hiring in-house IT staff, you get a full team of experts for a flat monthly fee. Corstar monitors your network, handles security, resolves issues, and keeps your technology running — so you don't have to.
We serve businesses throughout Westchester County, including White Plains, Yonkers, New Rochelle, Scarsdale, Mount Vernon, Tarrytown, Ossining, and surrounding communities. We also work with clients in nearby Rockland County and Fairfield County, CT.
Critical issues are addressed within 1–4 hours depending on your service plan. Routine requests are resolved within one business day. Our help desk is staffed during business hours, with after-hours emergency support available for clients on our Premium plan.
