The Foundation of Your Network

Your Cabling Is Your Network's Foundation

Every piece of software, every cloud service, every video call — it all runs on your physical network infrastructure. Poor cabling causes intermittent outages, slow performance, and security vulnerabilities that software can't fix.

Corstar has been installing structured cabling systems in Westchester County since 1970. We design, install, and certify cabling for every type of commercial environment — from small offices to multi-floor facilities and data closets.

Every installation is tested and certified with documentation you can hand to your landlord, insurance carrier, or next IT provider. We don't cut corners.

Cabling Services

  • Cat6 and Cat6A data cabling for workstations and access points
  • Fiber optic installation — multimode and single-mode
  • Network rack and patch panel installation and organization
  • VoIP phone cabling and installation
  • Wireless access point (WAP) cabling and placement
  • Security camera and IP surveillance cabling
  • Audio/visual and conference room infrastructure
  • Data center and server room cabling organization
  • Cable testing, certification, and as-built documentation
  • Remediation of legacy or non-compliant cabling systems
