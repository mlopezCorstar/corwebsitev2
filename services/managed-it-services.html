IT MSP Explained

What Is a Managed IT Service Provider?

A Managed Service Provider (MSP) takes full responsibility for your business technology — monitoring, maintenance, security, and support — for a predictable monthly fee. Instead of waiting for things to break, we keep them from breaking in the first place.

Corstar Communications has been Westchester County's IT MSP since 1970. We've seen every kind of IT failure, cyberattack, and technology transition imaginable. That experience is what you're getting when you work with us.

Whether you have 5 employees or 500, our managed IT plans scale to match your business. No hidden costs, no surprise invoices — just reliable technology and a team that answers when you call.

Service Coverage

Everything Included in Managed IT

Our managed IT service is not a basic help desk. It is a comprehensive technology partnership that covers your entire environment.

Proactive Monitoring & Maintenance

  • 24/7 network and server monitoring with automated alerting
  • Patch management — operating system, software, and firmware updates
  • Preventive maintenance and hardware health checks
  • Disk space, memory, and CPU performance tracking
  • Backup verification — we confirm your backups actually work
  • Monthly performance reports with plain-language summaries

Help Desk & On-Site Support

  • Staffed help desk during business hours — real people, not bots
  • Remote support resolves most issues within minutes
  • On-site dispatch for hardware, cabling, and hands-on issues
  • New employee setup and device provisioning
  • Printer, peripheral, and workstation support
  • Vendor coordination — we talk to your ISP, phone company, and software vendors for you

Cybersecurity Essentials

  • Managed endpoint detection and response (EDR)
  • Managed firewall configuration and monitoring
  • Email security — spam filtering and phishing protection
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) deployment
  • Dark web monitoring for compromised credentials
  • Security awareness training for your employees

Cloud & Productivity

  • Microsoft 365 administration — users, licenses, and policies
  • Cloud backup and disaster recovery
  • Azure infrastructure management
  • SharePoint and Teams configuration
  • VPN and secure remote access setup
  • SaaS application support and integration

Pricing Model

Simple, Predictable Pricing

We price our managed IT services per user, per month. No long-term lock-in on starter plans, no hidden project fees for routine work. You know exactly what you're paying every month.
