Microsoft 365

Microsoft 365 Migrations Done Right

Migrating to Microsoft 365 is one of the highest-impact IT moves a small or mid-size business can make. Done right, it gives your team better tools, stronger security, and the flexibility to work from anywhere. Done poorly, it causes email downtime, data loss, and employee frustration.

Corstar has migrated hundreds of Westchester businesses to Microsoft 365. We plan every migration carefully, run it over a weekend when possible, and ensure your team is ready to work on Monday morning.

After migration, we manage your Microsoft 365 tenant — user accounts, licensing, security policies, Teams and SharePoint configuration, and ongoing administration.
