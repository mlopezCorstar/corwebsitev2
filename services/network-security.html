The Threat Is Real

Westchester Businesses Are Being Targeted

Ransomware, phishing, and business email compromise are not abstract threats — they hit Westchester businesses every week. The average ransomware attack costs a small business over $200,000. Most never fully recover.

The problem is not that small businesses are careless. It's that cybercriminals know small businesses often lack the layered defenses that enterprises have. We change that equation.

Corstar's network security services give your business enterprise-grade protection at a price built for local businesses. We monitor, respond, and adapt so you don't have to.

What's Covered

  • Managed next-generation firewall (NGFW) configuration and monitoring
  • Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR) — advanced antivirus replacement
  • Email security — anti-phishing, anti-spam, and impersonation protection
  • DNS filtering — block malicious sites before they load
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) deployment across all accounts
  • Privileged access management — restrict who can do what on your network
  • Dark web credential monitoring — alerts if your passwords are leaked
  • Security awareness training — teach employees to spot phishing attacks
  • Vulnerability scanning — identify weaknesses before attackers do
  • Security incident response — rapid containment if a breach occurs
Our Approach

Layered Security — Not a Single Silver Bullet

No single tool stops every threat. We build defenses in layers so that if one fails, others catch what it missed.

🛡️

Perimeter

Next-gen firewalls and intrusion prevention at the network edge.

💻

Endpoint

EDR on every device — laptops, desktops, servers, and mobile.

📧

Identity

MFA, SSO, and email authentication stop credential theft cold.

🧠

Human Layer

Regular employee security training — because people are the last line of defense.
