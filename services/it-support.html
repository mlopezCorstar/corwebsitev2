Support That Actually Supports You

IT Support Built for Westchester Businesses

The difference between good IT support and bad IT support is simple: does someone who knows your business pick up the phone when you call?

At Corstar, your support requests go to a small team of engineers who are assigned to your account. They know your systems, your history, and your quirks. You won't spend 20 minutes explaining your setup every time you call.

We resolve most issues remotely in under an hour. When physical presence is required, we dispatch a technician to your Westchester location — typically the same or next business day.

What We Support

  • Windows and Mac workstations and laptops
  • Windows Server administration
  • Microsoft 365 — Outlook, Teams, SharePoint, OneDrive
  • Printers, scanners, and peripherals
  • VoIP phone systems
  • Line-of-business software (EHR, practice management, ERP, CRM)
  • Network connectivity troubleshooting
  • Password resets and account management
  • New employee setup and onboarding
  • Hardware procurement and deployment
