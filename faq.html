About Managed IT
An IT MSP (Managed Service Provider) is a company that takes over the management of your business technology. Instead of hiring an internal IT person or calling someone every time something breaks, you pay a flat monthly fee and get a full team of engineers who monitor your systems, handle security, and support your staff. Corstar Communications is a Westchester-based IT MSP that has served local businesses since 1970.
Our managed IT service covers proactive monitoring of your servers and network, automated patch management, managed endpoint security (antivirus replacement), email security, help desk support, on-site support when needed, Microsoft 365 administration, and monthly reporting. Cybersecurity essentials are included in every plan — not sold as a separate add-on.
Break-fix IT means you call someone when something is broken and pay by the hour. Managed IT means we watch your systems all the time and fix problems before they cause downtime. Most businesses find managed IT is less expensive over time because prevention is cheaper than emergency repair — and lost productivity from downtime is very costly.

Pricing & Contracts
We price managed IT per user, per month. For most small businesses in Westchester, comprehensive managed IT with security runs between $80–$150 per user per month. The actual price depends on your number of users, number of devices, your compliance requirements, and which plan tier fits your needs. We do not publish a price sheet because a business with 10 users has very different needs than one with 80. The best way to get an accurate number is to schedule a free assessment.
Our standard agreements are 12 months. This allows us to invest fully in understanding and optimizing your environment. We do offer flexible month-to-month arrangements for certain situations — ask us about it during your assessment.
